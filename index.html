Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services does FavouredChild Clinic provide?

    FavouredChild Clinic offers a comprehensive range of pediatric services including routine check-ups, vaccinations, developmental assessments, acute illness care, and specialized consultations for children of all ages. You can check our services page for more.

  • How can I schedule an appointment for my child?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our clinic during business hours or by using our online appointment booking system available on our website. We strive to accommodate urgent appointments as quickly as possible.

  • What should I do in case of a pediatric emergency outside of office hours?

    In case of a pediatric emergency outside of office hours, please call 911 or proceed to the nearest emergency room. For urgent medical concerns that do not require immediate attention, you can reach our on-call provider by calling our office number.
